MATH IS FUN!

This is a project built to improve math skills. To test a function open console in the dev tools of your browser. You may need to use Google Chrome if your browser does not have this functionality. Once in the console you can call any formula and enter your paramaters. It should respond with your answer as an alert box in your browser. Enjoy!
